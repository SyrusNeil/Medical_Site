COVID-19

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus.

Most people infected with the virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment.

However, some will become seriously ill and require medical attention.

Older people and those with underlying medical conditions like cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, or cancer are more likely to develop serious illness.

Anyone can get sick with COVID-19 and become seriously ill or die at any age.

Symptoms

1.Fever

2.Tiredness

3.Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing

4.Muscle aches

5.Sore throat

6.Chest pain

7.Vomiting

8.Diarrhea

9.Pink eye (conjunctivitis)

Risk Factors

Risk factors for COVID-19 include:

1.Age. Your risk increases as you get older.

2.Chronic Issues. Such as heart failure, chronic kidney issues, asthma, neumonia, diabetes etc.

3.Smoking. Smoking increases the effects of COVID-19 as it reduces the lifespan of your lungs.

4.Close Contact. Close contact (within 6 feet, or 2 meters) with someone who has COVID-19

5.Transmission. Being coughed or sneezed on by an infected person

Prevention

Prevention methods include:

1.Taking 2 doses of the vaccine.

2.Additional dose.

3.Booster dose.

4. Second booster dose.
